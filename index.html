How to use the timer

Type of egg: Large eggs (~50 - 55g) & Fridge-cold

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil.
  2. Once the water has boiled, lower the heat such that the water is at a gentle boil.
  3. Select the type of egg that you prefer: Runny yolks, Soft-boiled eggs, Hard-boiled eggs.
  4. Place the eggs gently into the water.
  5. Click on start to start the timer.
  6. Once the timer is up, transfer the egg into a large boil of cold water.
  7. Voila! Your perfect egg is done!
